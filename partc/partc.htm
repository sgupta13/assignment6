Rules to Play Boutique of Flowers pairs matching game

1) You should select the number of pictures on the game board and the number of seconds the board will be visible before hiding the tiles
2) The actual picture tiles will be visible to you to memorize and then fliped
3) You will be given 120 seconds for 8 photos pair, 150 seconds for 10 photos pair, and 180 seconds for 12 photos for pairs. 4) The timer will start and then you will match the pairs
4) The pairs which match will remain visible and tiles that do not match will be hidden again.
5) Game will be over if time lapses
